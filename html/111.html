This is a hub for the analysis, writing, and results of my NBA survey. It consists of 46 questions, split into 5 sections: 10 Mentality questions about topics such as tanking, clutchness, and advanced stats, 14 Rules questions including topics such as conference imbalance, flopping, and hack-a-fouls, 6 Hypothetical Questions on topics such as an injury-free 2015 NBA Finals, a suspension-free 2016 NBA Finals, and an only retired once MJ, 10 Player Questions on the places in history of LeBron James, Kobe Bryant, Stephen Curry and more, and 6 Demographic Questions.

I've been lucky enough to get over 1250 responses. Thanks to all for getting through and supporting it. Any feedback is welcome at dannyliang.726@gmail.com
